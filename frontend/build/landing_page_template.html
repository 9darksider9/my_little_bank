What is My Little Bank?

My Little Bank is an educational platform designed to teach children about financial literacy through a fun and interactive banking simulation.

Features

  • Virtual bank accounts for kids
  • Budgeting tools
  • Savings goals
  • Educational games and quizzes
  • Parent dashboard for oversight

How it works

  1. Parents create a family account
  2. Children get their own virtual bank accounts
  3. Kids earn virtual money through tasks and good financial habits
  4. Children learn to budget, save, and make financial decisions
  5. Parents oversee and guide their children's financial journey

Benefits for kids

  • Learn financial responsibility in a safe environment
  • Develop good money habits early
  • Gain confidence in managing finances
  • Have fun while learning important life skills

Benefits for parents

  • Teach children about money management
  • Monitor and guide children's financial decisions
  • Prepare kids for real-world financial challenges
  • Bond with children through financial education
  • Your children's information is only seen by you, NO ONE else has access
  • This allows you to 'pay' allowances without having to hand out money
